    Why Coding Temple?

    Prior to any interest in coding I was working in sales. I was able to learn a lot from working in that industry, however at the end of the day it wasn't for me. I had two friends who attended Coding Temple that recommended it to me since I always had an interest in technology. After seeing the direction the world was headed toward, it seemed like a great idea. My goal is to become a software developer, however that can change since I don't know too deeply about this industry. However, after doing the precourse work and self studying I found it very fun and interesting to learn and make progress.
